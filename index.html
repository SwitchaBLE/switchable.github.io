What is SwitchaBLE?

alarm clock app device design

The SwitchaBLE hardware goes between a lightbulb and its socket.

The iPhone/Android app allows you to set an alarm on your phone.

When it's time to wake up,

your phone sends a signal to the SwitchaBLE,

which flashes your lights.
